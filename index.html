Data Model Comparison - OAP Nepal Pilot Contract Data Model vs. Open Contracting Draft Standard

From March-June, 2013, Development Gateway, the Open Aid Partnership, and the Government of Nepal worked on a pilot exercise to collect and code procurement data. The goal of that exercise was to see the degree to which open procurement data could provide operationally-relevant information on development activities, with the view of aiding planners and policy makers better understand patterns of spending in their sectors/countries.

This webpage is meant as a rough working document to help undertake an open comparison between the Draft Open Contracting data model and the data model used in the Nepal pilot. This is not meant as a formal publication of the Open Aid Partnership, the Government of Nepal, or Development Gateway. For any questions about this webpage, or to request more formal documentation on the pilot, please contact Owen Scott at < oscott (at) developmentgateway.org. >
